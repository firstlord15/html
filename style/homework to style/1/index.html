Watch your Head

Ultimate Frisbee is a team non-contact sports game using a flying disc ("Frisbee"). It was originally known as ultimate Frisbee. Over time, due to the fact that the word frisbee is not only a common name for flying discs, but also a trademark of the company "Wham-O", the name was shortened to ultimate (ultimate). In the name "ultimate Frisbee", the word ultimate is a non-declinable adjective: ultimate Frisbee competition, but the name "ultimate" is a declinable noun: ultimate competition.
